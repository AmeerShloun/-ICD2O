Steps for infinite pie

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Put it in the oven
  3. burn the food and repeat the steps!

And take a peak at my table for more detail

Table of Ingredients, When to Use Them, and The Results

Ingredients What to do Result
Milk Mix it in the bowl to bake Burn and try again
Dough Mix into the bowl, use some of it for ribbons, and bake Burn it and try again
Apples Chop them up and mix, then bake it Burn it and try again
Flour Mix it in, probably sift first though, in order to bake Burn it and try again
Sugar Mix it into the bowl in order to bake Burn it and try again
